Can I Comma Delimit Multiple Rows Into One Column?top_rated_posts_029

I/O Example

INPUT: input1

TicketID:StrPerson:Str
T0001Alice
T0001Bob
T0002Catherine
T0002Doug
T0003Elaine

OUTPUT

TicketID:StrPeople:Str
T0001Alice Bob
T0002Catherine Doug
T0003Elaine

Hints

  • (empty)

Our Solution


SELECT
    TicketID,
    string_agg(Person, ' ') 
FROM
    input1 
GROUP BY
    TicketID 
ORDER BY
    TicketID ASC
